<p><p>The needle-free extension set market is categorized into single-lumen (SL) and double-lumen (DL) types. Single-lumen extension sets feature one channel for fluid delivery, enhancing simplicity and reducing infection risk. They are ideal for straightforward applications. In contrast, double-lumen extension sets have two separate channels, allowing simultaneous administration of multiple infusions or medications without cross-contamination. This versatility caters to more complex medical scenarios, making DL sets suitable for patients requiring multifaceted treatment protocols. Both types enhance safety and efficiency in healthcare settings.</p></p>

<p><p>The compostable toothbrush market can be categorized into two main types: fully compostable toothbrushes and handle compostable toothbrushes. Fully compostable toothbrushes are made entirely from biodegradable materials, allowing the entire product to break down in composting environments. In contrast, handle compostable toothbrushes feature handles made from compostable materials but may have nylon bristles that are not biodegradable. Both types aim to reduce plastic waste in dental hygiene products, promoting environmentally friendly alternatives for consumers.</p></p>

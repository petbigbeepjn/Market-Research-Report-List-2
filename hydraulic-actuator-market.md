<p><strong>The Hydraulic Actuator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Actuators</li><li>Rotary Actuators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic actuator market consists of two primary types: linear actuators and rotary actuators. Linear actuators convert hydraulic energy into straight-line motion, making them ideal for applications requiring precise positioning and force, such as in industrial machinery and automation. Rotary actuators, on the other hand, provide rotational motion by utilizing hydraulic pressure to drive gears or vanes, suitable for tasks like controlling valves or operating heavy equipment. Both types are essential in various industries, including manufacturing, construction, and aerospace.</p></p>

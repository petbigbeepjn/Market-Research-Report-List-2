<p><p>The petroleum resins market is categorized into several types based on their chemical structure and composition. C5 resins, derived from C5 hydrocarbons, are known for their light color and durability, making them suitable for adhesives and coatings. C9 resins, derived from C9 hydrocarbons, offer excellent adhesion and heat resistance, often used in inks and sealants. C5/C9 resins combine properties of both types, enhancing versatility. Hydrogenated resins, produced through hydrogenation processes, offer improved stability and lower odor, catering to high-performance applications.</p></p>

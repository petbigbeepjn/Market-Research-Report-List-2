<p><p>MLCC (Multi-Layer Ceramic Capacitors) are crucial in the new energy automotive market, specifically in low and high capacitance segments. Low capacitance MLCCs, typically below 1μF, are utilized for filtering and decoupling in electronic control units, ensuring stable power supply and signal integrity. Conversely, high capacitance MLCCs, often exceeding 1μF, are essential for energy storage in applications like regenerative braking and powertrains. Both types enhance system efficiency, reliability, and performance, contributing significantly to the advancement of electric and hybrid vehicles.</p></p>

<p><p>The automatic curling iron market is primarily divided into two types: automatic steam curling irons and automatic air curling irons. Automatic steam curling irons utilize steam technology to hydrate and curl hair simultaneously, enhancing shine and reducing heat damage. In contrast, automatic air curling irons rely on integrated airflow to create curls without direct heat contact, promoting hair health while offering versatility in curl tightness. Both types cater to consumers seeking convenient and efficient hairstyling solutions, emphasizing ease of use and hair protection.</p></p>

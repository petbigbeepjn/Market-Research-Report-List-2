<p><strong>The Fire Extinguisers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stored Pressure Type</li><li>Cartridge-Operated Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fire extinguisher market primarily features two types: stored pressure and cartridge-operated extinguishers. Stored pressure types are pre-filled with extinguishing agents and maintain pressure to ensure immediate use. They are typically lighter and more convenient for quick access. In contrast, cartridge-operated extinguishers utilize a separate cartridge to create pressure, allowing for more controlled release of the extinguishing agent. This type is often preferred for larger, more specialized applications, providing flexibility in operation and maintenance.</p></p>

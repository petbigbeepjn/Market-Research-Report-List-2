<p><p>The mulch applicators market consists of two primary types: coating mulch applicators and pre-coated mulch applicators. Coating mulch applicators are designed to apply a layer of mulch material directly onto the soil, enhancing moisture retention and weed suppression. In contrast, pre-coated mulch applicators utilize mulch that is already treated or embedded with nutrients or pesticides, allowing for easier application and improved soil health. These technologies cater to diverse agricultural needs, improving efficiency and crop yields.</p></p>

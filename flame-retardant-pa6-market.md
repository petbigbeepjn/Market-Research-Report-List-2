<p><p>The Flame Retardant PA6 market is categorized into two main types: Halogen Type and Halogen-Free Type. Halogen Type flame retardants contain elements like chlorine or bromine, providing effective fire resistance but raising environmental and health concerns. In contrast, Halogen-Free Type flame retardants use alternative materials such as phosphorous or inorganic compounds, offering safer options with less toxic byproducts and reduced environmental impact. Both types are utilized in various applications, including automotive, electronics, and construction to enhance safety and compliance.</p></p>

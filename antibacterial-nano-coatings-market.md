<p><p>The antibacterial nano coatings market features several key types of deposition techniques. Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) involves creating coatings from gaseous precursors, allowing for uniform coverage on complex surfaces. Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) utilizes physical methods to evaporate materials, producing thin films with high purity. Atomic Layer Deposition (ALD) offers precise control at the atomic level for layered coatings. Other methods may include sol-gel processes and spray coating, catering to diverse applications across industries like healthcare and electronics.</p></p>

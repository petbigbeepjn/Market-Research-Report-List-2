<p><p>Torque angle sensors are vital for measuring the rotational position and angle in various applications. The market can be divided into two main types: contact and non-contact sensors. Contact type sensors rely on direct mechanical interaction with the rotating component, providing precise measurements but may face wear over time. In contrast, non-contact type sensors utilize technologies like magnetic or optical methods to measure torque without physical contact, offering enhanced durability and less maintenance, ideal for demanding environments.</p></p>

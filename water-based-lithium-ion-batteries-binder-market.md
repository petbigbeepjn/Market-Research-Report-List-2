Anode binders are used to bind active materials in the anode, ensuring effective electron flow and stability during charge and discharge cycles. Cathode binders serve a similar purpose for cathode materials, enhancing structural integrity and conductivity. The shift towards water-based binders is driven by environmental concerns and the need for safer, non-toxic alternatives, making these binders crucial for sustainable battery production.</p></p>

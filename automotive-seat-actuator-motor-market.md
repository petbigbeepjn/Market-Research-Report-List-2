<p><ul><li>Two-pole Motor</li><li>Four-pole Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive seat actuator motor market is largely divided into two main types: two-pole motors and four-pole motors. Two-pole motors are typically smaller, lighter, and more cost-effective, making them suitable for basic seat adjustment functions. In contrast, four-pole motors offer higher torque and efficiency, allowing for more complex movements and features such as lumbar support and heated seats. The choice between these motor types often depends on the specific requirements of the vehicle's seating system and consumer preferences.</p></p>

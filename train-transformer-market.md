<p><p>The train transformer market encompasses various types essential for railway operations. Traction transformers convert electrical energy for train propulsion. Auxiliary transformers provide power for auxiliary systems like lighting and ventilation. Booster transformers enhance voltage and power levels for efficient performance on long-distance routes. Isolation transformers ensure electrical safety by isolating the train's electrical systems from the main supply. Together, these transformers play a critical role in enhancing efficiency, safety, and reliability in the railway sector.</p></p>

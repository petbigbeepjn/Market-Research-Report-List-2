<p><p>The Home Smart Vent market is segmented into three primary types: floor-embedded, ceiling-embedded, and wall-embedded systems. Floor-embedded vents are integrated into the flooring, providing discreet airflow while maintaining aesthetic appeal. Ceiling-embedded vents are installed in ceilings, effectively distributing air without occupying wall or floor space. Wall-embedded vents are placed within the walls, allowing for efficient heating and cooling in a convenient location. Each type caters to different architectural designs and homeowner preferences while enhancing indoor climate control.</p></p>

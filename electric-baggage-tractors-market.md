<p><p>The electric baggage tractor market includes two primary battery types: lead-acid and lithium-ion (Li-ion). Lead-acid batteries are traditional, widely used for their lower initial cost and reliability, though they are heavier and have a shorter lifespan. In contrast, Li-ion batteries offer higher energy density, lighter weight, and longer life, making them increasingly popular in modern electric baggage tractors. The choice between these battery types significantly impacts operational efficiency, maintenance costs, and overall performance in airport logistics.</p></p>

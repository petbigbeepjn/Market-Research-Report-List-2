<p><p>Carbon thermoplastic (CFRTP) composites are classified primarily into two types based on the carbon fiber precursor: PAN (Polyacrylonitrile) and Pitch. PAN-based CFRTPs are known for their superior mechanical properties and are widely used in aerospace and automotive applications due to their strength and lightweight characteristics. Pitch-based CFRTPs, on the other hand, offer excellent thermal conductivity and are generally more suitable for electrical applications. Both types enhance performance and expand the usability of composite materials across various industries.</p></p>
